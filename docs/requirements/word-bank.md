# Word Bank

## User Story

As a learner, when I tap a word I don't know, I want it automatically added to my word bank so I can revisit it later.

---

## Requirements

### Adding Words
- [ ] Tapping a word for the first time automatically adds it to the word bank
- [ ] If the word already exists in the word bank, it is not added again as a duplicate

### Tracking Encounters
- [ ] When a word that is already in the word bank is tapped, the word bank records that it was encountered again
- [ ] The word bank tracks how many times each word has been encountered
- [ ] The word bank records when each word was last encountered
